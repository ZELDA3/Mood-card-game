Mood Card Game

How to Play

  • Goal:Match all pairs of mood cards before time runs out!
  • Memorize:You'll get 3 seconds to study all cards before they flip
  • Match:Click two cards to reveal them , if they match you get 10 points!
  • Level up:Score 30+ points ( 3 matches) to unlock Level 2
  • Lives:You have 3 lives , wrong match cost 1 life
  • Timer:15 seconds per level , beat the clock!
  • Win:Complete both levels to rank on the leaderboard
