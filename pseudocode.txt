the aim of the game is to match cards think about it as memory cards we all used to play.
to get into the game data you need to enter a username.
usernames are required to qualify players into entering the top 10 leaderboard.
instructions of the game are written above the username input box.
after reading the instructions and picking up a username the player will hit the "START GAME" button to launch the game.
Players will have three seconds to study the cards before they are flipped over so they try to memorize their positions.
those cards are randomized each start/reset.
players can check their progress looking at the panel under the title which has the Score, Lives and Timer.
if the player matched messageDisplay will show "MATCHED"!.
the player need to match at least 3 pairs to get 30 in score which will upgrade them to level two.
each round will have 16 cards listed (which are doubled from the main 8 characters).
players only get a set of 3 lives and 15sec to win each level.
if the player loses the display will show : GAME OVER!
if the player wins the display will show : YOU WON!
